Rules & Information


How to Play

  1. Set the number of players and fill in player names.
  2. Choose a category or leave blank for random selection.
  3. Select a lineup type.
  4. Decide if you want Snake Draft mode.
  5. Click "Start Draft" to begin drafting characters/items for each position.
  6. Once all positions are filled, click "Finish Draft" to generate the final prompt.

Tips

  • Snake Draft: Turns alternate order each round for fair picks.
  • Baseball lineups require correct batting orders.
  • Duplicate character/item selections are not allowed.
  • Use the generated prompt to evaluate your lineup in ChatGPT or other apps.
